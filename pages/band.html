The Flobots

The Flobots in Tucson, Arizona on May 16, 2008.

The Flobots are a rap group from Denver, CO, with influences from rappers, like the Roots and Common, and alternative hard rock bands like Rage Against the Machine and Tool. The band originated in 2005, releasing its first recording in October of 2005. The band is unique in that it has two MCs , or mic controllers, instead of the usual one. Their most popular song, known as "Handlebars", was released in 2008, and mixed Rock and Rap. This platinum-plus smash hit pushed them into the mainstream.
